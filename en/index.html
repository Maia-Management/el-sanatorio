What it is

An immersive narrative experience built around the real history of the Hospital San Juan de Dios and the ghost of La Monja del Pasillo. You'll be admitted as a patient, processed by Dr. Silvio's compounding pharmacy, and walked through the wards. Some of it is theatre. Some of it is real history. You decide which is which.

Inside the experience: a working bar (La Farmacia) where Dr. Silvio "compounds" the drinks himself, a yakitori grill (Chuzo Tokyo) on the street frontage, and a series of rooms that re-enact a 1958 sanatorium scandal that the regional health authority quietly closed.

The full site, the booking flow, and our receptionist Hortensia all speak Spanish primarily. If you'd like to book in English, WhatsApp us directly — we'll handle the reservation by hand. Spanish-speaking and bilingual customers can use the main site below.
