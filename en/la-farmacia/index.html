What's at the bar

Prescriptions — house cocktails served from sterile-looking syringe taps. Dr. Silvio compounds them himself. The IV drip on the wall is structural.

Chuzo Tokyo yakitori — charcoal-grilled skewers from a counter on Calle 19. Walk-in, no reservation, until the brasa runs out.

Sushi Pop — Caribbean-Japanese sushi by the slice and the box. Same kitchen that supplies the Rappi delivery network across the Caribbean coast.

Non-alcoholic — re-hydration solutions, sodas, and one virgin cocktail per session per the doctor's orders.

Our menu, our receptionist Hortensia, and the booking flow all speak Spanish primarily. If you'd like to reserve in English, WhatsApp us directly — we'll handle it by hand. Or browse the English menu below.
