3. Purposes of Processing

Your personal data will be processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Managing, confirming and administering table and experience bookings.
  • Responding to requests, enquiries and petitions sent through the site or via WhatsApp.
  • Sending booking confirmations and reminders.
  • With your express authorisation: sending commercial communications about events, promotions and venue news.
  • Complying with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Measuring website performance (only if you accept analytics cookies).

4. Authorisation and Consent (Art. 9, Law 1581 of 2012)

Under Article 9 of Law 1581 of 2012, the processing of personal data requires the prior, express and informed authorisation of the data subject. By ticking the consent box on the booking form, you grant this authorisation. Authorisation may be revoked at any time by written notice to legal@el-sanatorio.com.

For analytics cookies, authorisation is granted via the consent banner shown when you first enter the site.

5. Rights of the Data Subject (Art. 8, Law 1581 of 2012)

As the subject of your personal data, you have the right to:

  • Know the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update and correct your data when it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Request proof of the authorisation you granted.
  • Be informed of the use that has been made of your personal data.
  • File complaints with Colombia’s Superintendency of Industry and Commerce (SIC) for breaches of the law.
  • Revoke authorisation and/or request deletion of your data, except where a legal duty to retain it applies.
  • Free access to your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact the data controller: EL SANATORIO S.A.S., email: legal@el-sanatorio.com, phone: +1 903 459 8763. We will respond within the time limits set by law (up to 15 business days for enquiries and 15 business days for claims).

6. Sharing of Data

The personal data we collect will not be sold, assigned or transferred to third parties for commercial purposes without your consent. Data may only be shared with:

  • Netlify, Inc. (hosting provider, USA), as a data processor for form reception and routing.
  • Supabase Inc. (database provider, USA), as a data processor for storing booking, casting and contact form submissions. Data is encrypted at rest and only the authorised Maia Management team can query it.
  • Google LLC (web analytics provider, USA), only if you accept analytics cookies.
  • Competent Colombian authorities, where there is a legal obligation.

All international data transfers are made under standard contractual clauses or adequate-protection arrangements pursuant to Article 26 of Law 1581 of 2012 and Decree 1377 of 2013.

7. Data Retention

Booking data is retained for 24 months from the date of the booking, or until the data subject requests deletion. Web analytics data is anonymous and does not allow identification of natural persons.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The website el-sanatorio.com uses the following technologies:

  • Essential cookies: Store your consent preference. Do not require authorisation.
  • Google Analytics: Web-traffic measurement tool. Only loads after your explicit consent for analytics cookies.

You can withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and reloading the site. Your preference is stored in localStorage under the key maia_consent.

9. Security

EL SANATORIO S.A.S. implements reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss or destruction, including the use of HTTPS (TLS) for all site communications.

10. Minors

El Sanatorio is a nighttime entertainment venue that serves alcohol. The website is not directed at people under 18 and does not knowingly collect data from minors. If you are under 18, you should not use the booking form or the services of this venue.
